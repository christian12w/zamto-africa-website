Who We Are

Zamto Africa Company Ltd stands as Zambia's premier destination for authentic Japanese imported vehicles. Founded with a vision to revolutionize the automotive industry in Zambia, we bring together decades of expertise and a passion for excellence.

Our commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction has made us the trusted choice for hundreds of Zambians seeking reliable, premium vehicles from Japan.

Our Mission

To provide Zambians with access to high-quality, authentic Japanese vehicles while delivering unparalleled customer service and building lasting relationships based on trust and excellence.
