Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum down payment required?

Down payments range from 15-50% depending on the financing option and your credit profile. Our dealer financing offers as low as 30% down payment.

How long does the approval process take?

With Zamto Africa financing, approval can be within 24 hours. Bank loans typically take 3-5 business days for processing.

Can I pay off my loan early?

Yes, our dealer financing allows early repayment without penalties. Bank loans may have early repayment fees - check with your specific bank.

What if I have a poor credit history?

We have flexible options for various credit situations. A larger down payment or shorter loan term may help secure approval.
